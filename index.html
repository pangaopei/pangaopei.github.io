           I am a PhD Student of the Institute of Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IOP, CAS) and will graduate in June 2023. My supervisor is Zi Yang Meng, Associate Professor of University of Hong Kong.
          My primary research interests are model design and numerical investigations using quantum Monte Carlo (QMC) method, to study quantum criticality, non-Fermi liquid (nFL), quantum morie models and twistronics. I'm also interested in algorithms developments and Sign Problem in QMC simulation.
           More information about my research can be found elsewhere on the website: Publications, CV, and Research Statement
